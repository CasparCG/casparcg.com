Pre-rendered video and audio is normally played via the [FFmpeg Producer](../server/producers/ffmpeg-producer.md) directly from the file system.

An alternative is to embed audio and/or video in an FT file and play with the Flash Producer.

## Video Codecs

The FFmpeg Producer can play all the codecs that [FFmpeg](http://ffmpeg.org/) supports.

## Sample Videos

The CasparCG [download page](http://casparcg.com/download/) has links to several packages with sample video files.
